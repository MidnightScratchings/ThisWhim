This Whim is a maze game based on the love story of two characters. The setup in simple: the two characters are placed in a maze, the player controlling the black square and the computer controlling the circle. Hidden in the maze is a rose, and you (the player) must find it to give to your love (the circle) before they find you. (to know more of how to play the game, visit the 'Instructions' tab above)

The basis of the story is that you are a character too nervous to approach your crush (sounds familiar...). You find yourself in a maze with your love and decide to act on your whims and impress them. In the maze is a rose, sufficient to do the job. However, if you do not get a flower for your love, you'll faint when confronted by your them.
